our Journey

1. Epignotects Architecture Ltd.

Founded as Epignotects Architecture Ltd., our firm quickly established itself as a prominent provider of architectural solutions in Accra, Ghana, earning a reputation for excellence, creativity, and client satisfaction.

2. Recognizing the Soul's Impact

As our firm evolved, so too has our vision and mission. Recognizing the profound impact that architecture can have on the human spirit, we have rebranded as Crafted Habitat Architecture to better embody our commitment to creating spaces that nurture the soul.

3. Embracing the Crafted Habitat Vision

While our name has changed, our dedication to excellence and our passion for soulful design remain unaltered. As we embark on this new chapter, we are excited about the opportunities that lie ahead, poised to continue shaping the built environment in ways that inspire and enrich the lives of all who inhabit our spaces.
