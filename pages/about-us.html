ABOUT US

Who we are
Welcome to CRAFT’D HABITAT Architecture, where we believe in the transformative power of architecture to enrich the soul and elevate the human experience. Formerly known as Epignotects Architecture Ltd, we have rebranded to better reflect our commitment to creating spaces that nurture holistic wellbeing and inspire personal growth.
An interplay of nature and thoughtful design nurtures the soul.

Mission: Creating personalized sanctuaries
Inspiring Creativity - Our mission is to create personalized sanctuaries that inspire creativity, foster wellbeing, and facilitate high-quality decision-making for our clients and the communities they serve.

Nurturing wellbeing - We go beyond aesthetics to prioritize the holistic wellbeing of our clients, designing environments that support physical, emotional, and spiritual health, promoting personal growth and overall happiness.

Enriching the Soul - At the heart of our mission is a deep commitment to creating spaces that enrich the soul, incorporating elements that promote peace, inspiration, and a connection with the natural world.

Facilitating decision making - Our spaces are tailored to the unique needs of high-level executives and decision-makers, providing a retreat from the demands of their fast-paced careers and fostering an atmosphere conducive to clear thinking and impactful decision-making.

The Crafted Habitat Difference
Soulful Design – spaces can evolve into sanctuaries of serenity interplay of nature and thoughtful design nurturs the soul.
Holistic wellbeing – a shift that explicitly puts peoples wellness at the centre of the conception, design, and creation of our homes and neighbourhoods
Biophilia Integration- Incorporating plants and nature into both design and experience is a key element of true wellness architecture. Plants are proven to enhance cognitive performance, cleanse the air, lower stress and reduce toxins.
Advanced Technology- We use AI to quickly generate images. We move with speed. We empower clients to visualize before project is done
