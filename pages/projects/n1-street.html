N1 Street Villa Project

Dzorwulu, Accra-Ghana, 2019

This family residence is a must-have abode for impeccable detail and quality. With wide open spaces and beautiful finishing, be prepared to be captivated from the very first.

Crafted Habitat was appointed by Mr Charles in January 2019 to develop a 5-bedroom luxury residence with staff outhouse on one and half plot size.

The site is by a side road off the major N1 Highway at Djorwulu, a high residential hub in Accra. The main component of the brief was to incorporate a double volume living room, and have enough daylighting throughout the entire building and in so doing, find opportunities to organise the plan/ layout.
