Toyota Ghana Ltd Flagship Showroom

Accra, Ghana 2018

This flagship showroom proposal was designed to broaden and engage customer experience by using cutting edge technology.

Date begun:
2018

Completion date
-


Location
Accra-Ghana

Plan

Proposed Contemporary Remodel

Digital display screens, interactive tablets and power walls span across the display room allowing customer to have access to digital vehicle display beyond the walls of the showroom.

Ceiling design, targeted lighting and colour variation was interactively used to give give special emphasis to the vehicles in the showroom, making each car a ‘superstar’.
