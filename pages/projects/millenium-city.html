Millenium City Apartment

Weija, Accra-Ghana, 2017

Millennium City Estate Development is a new developing residential enclave located off the Kasoa Winneba road, in the Central Region of Ghana.

A 0.23 acre plot located at the Millennium City Estates was to be developed into a modern Serviced Apartment that would first serve Kasoa and its catchment areas about 40 minute’s drive from central Accra

Crafted Habitat was consulted to design this commercial block that would serve as a serviced apartment for guest in the city.
